In 2015, Madonna surprised the world when she revealed that she had once dated Tupac Shakur. Speaking about her relationship with Shakur, Madonna said, “I was dating Tupac Shakur at the time, and the thing is he got me all riled up on life in general.” A love letter dated January 15, 1995, at 4:30 a.m from the rapper to the pop icon also shed lights on their relationship. The letter was set to go up for auction at Gotta Have Rock and Roll with a starting bid of $100,000.
